Kentaro Miura 		Berserk (1988) 9.6/10

Its 300+ chapters take quite while to get trough(only 10% of the fandom actually has done so), but every second is worth it. Beautiful story about struggle, friendship, and humanity. Has the same vibe the games it inspired, soulslikes have: David vs Goliath, but Goliath is a monstrous abomination, and David is a badass guy with a huge sword.

Rest in peace, Miura.


Tatsuki Fujimoto 		Chainsaw Man (2018) 9.7/10

THE CAST. the cast. all characters are unique in personality, and have deep development. unlike most other shonen, chainsaw man succeeds to be more than just cool battle scenes. the quality of the story is what makes it unique among other titles in the medium.

peak. I am in love with this manga. The nihilstic story, the existential dread, the plot twists, the ag(o)ny. There has never been a more relatable character made than Togata.
